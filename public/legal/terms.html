Pattern Coach Terms

Effective date: May 17, 2026

Pattern Coach is a chess training and analysis tool. Engine evaluations, ratings, and recommendations are educational estimates and may be wrong.

Acceptable Use

Only analyze public games, your own games, or PGNs you have permission to use. Do not use the app to violate Chess.com or tournament rules.

No Competitive Assistance

Do not use Pattern Coach during live games or events where outside assistance is forbidden.

Availability

Chess.com sync depends on Chess.com's public API availability and rate limits. Local PGN analysis remains available without a Chess.com account.
