Pattern Coach Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026

Pattern Coach analyzes public Chess.com games and PGN text that you choose to provide. The app stores your Chess.com username, sync preferences, imported analysis report, and training state locally on your device.

Data We Use

We use your Chess.com username to request public game archives from Chess.com. We do not ask for your Chess.com password. PGN text you paste or upload is processed for analysis.

Local Storage

Your profile settings and reports are stored locally so the app can reopen with your latest training dashboard. You can clear local data from the app menu.

Third Parties

The app connects to Chess.com's public API to retrieve public game data for the username you enter. Do not enter usernames or PGNs you do not have permission to analyze.

Tracking and Ads

The app does not include advertising, third-party tracking, or cross-app tracking.

Contact

For privacy requests or support, use the support contact listed on the app's App Store page.
